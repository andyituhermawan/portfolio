Data Scientist

I have several years of experience as a teacher, and then decided to shift my career to become a data scientist consultant and lecturer.

My expertise includes data preprocessing, feature engineering, model development, and evaluation, ensuring that predictive analytics contribute to business efficiency and risk mitigation.

As a lecturer, I integrate industry knowledge with academic insights, helping students and professionals bridge the gap between theoretical learning and practical applications in data science.
